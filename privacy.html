Amazon Ads History Exporter — Privacy Policy

Milton Styles LLC  ·  Effective March 2026

The bottom line This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything happens inside your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

What This Extension Does

Amazon Ads History Exporter is a Chrome extension that reads the History tab (Campaign level or Ad Group level) already displayed in your Amazon Ads and saves it as a CSV file on your computer. That's it.

When you click "Export to CSV," the extension scrolls through the full list on screen, captures every row, and triggers a download directly to your device. No data is sent anywhere else.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not collect your Amazon account credentials, username, or password
  • We do not collect your Amazon session tokens or cookies
  • We do not record or store your browsing history
  • We do not collect or store the advertising data you export
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking of any kind

How Your Data Is Handled

All processing happens locally inside your browser. The extension reads what is already visible on your screen, assembles it into a CSV file, and downloads it to your computer using your browser's standard file download.

The extension uses chrome.storage.local only to temporarily store export state (for example, whether an export is currently running). This data never leaves your device and is cleared automatically.

The CSV file produced is downloaded directly to your computer. Milton Styles LLC never receives, sees, or stores this file.

Permissions Explained

The extension requests only the minimum permissions needed to function:

PermissionWhy it is needed
activeTab To read the Change History table on the Amazon Ads tab you have open
downloads To trigger the CSV file download to your computer
scripting To run the export logic on the Amazon Advertising page
storage To store temporary export state locally in your browser
Host: advertising.amazon.com To limit the extension to only run on Amazon Advertising pages — nowhere else

Third Parties

This extension does not share any data with any third party. It has no backend servers, no analytics service, and no external API dependencies. There are no third-party SDKs embedded in the extension.
